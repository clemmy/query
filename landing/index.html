About US

Query, A Peer-Reviewed Learning Solution Committed to helping students get the most out of their education.

Query is revolutionizing the way teachers teach and students learn. From the seats of college classrooms to the halls of large conferences and conventions, Query is capturing unaddressed questions of individuals and educators by compiling them onto an integrated platform. With a simple text message, individuals can be sure their questions, concerns, or comments can be addressed.
Ask us how!

- Classroom Focus
In today's society, students don't have the luxury of small group lectures or quality interaction with faculty. Instead, they face jam-packed classrooms, shared study space, and overwhelming amounts of information. While there are small, luxurious moments of one-on-one quality time, there is a limited amount of contributions and homogenous flow of ideas in overcrowded and competitive spaces. Reports show that students often refrain from asking questions and contributing ideas due to:

  • » Fear of a "Stupid" Question
  • » Speed of lecture and Limited time
  • » Language Barriers
  • » Question of Relevance
  • » Falling Behind
  • » Expectations
  • » Other

Query addresses these issues by allowing users to text their comments and questions during lectures over a hosted web app. In between or after lectures, professors can review can open the stored questions and choose relevant and important ideas that were brought up. Additionally, students can upvote particular questions or comments they find meaningful; further giving the professor a gauge of class sentiment. Query boosts student involvement and information retention, empowering students and improving academic outcomes. Giving students an interactive application on their mobile devices benefits both students and professors.
+ Conference and Networking Application
People attend conferences to learn, network with others, and to become inspired. Some indidviduals use it as an opportunity to solicit business while others share information. Regardless of the intention, conferences harbor audiences with unique backgrounds and input. Harnessing the value of individual contributions is a key metric and indicator of successful meetings. Even more so than academic conferences, meetings held in industry are excellent opportunities to network and share information. Query's ability to store input during keynote speakers and presenters is a powerful tool for integrating attendee sentiment and involvement.
+ Future Implications
Query has potential to expand into professional events, conventions, and mass media venues. It serves as a real time data collection tool that can be used to improve large-scale events.
